About CH24

Not a perfume brand. A fragrance house.

CH24 was built with one clear intention: to bring a fresh, Gen Z perspective into India's fragrance market. A category dominated by legacy brands, heavy glass bottles, and occasion-only thinking.

We saw a generation that moves differently. They carry less. They want to smell intentional every single day, not just on Friday nights. They want fragrance that fits in a pocket, not on a shelf. A generation no legacy fragrance brand in India was actually speaking to.

So we built CH24. Not a cheaper version of an existing perfume brand. Not a mass-market deo with better packaging. Something completely new: a fragrance house designed from the ground up for the way Gen Z lives, carries, and identifies with scent.

The format is the message

We started with Take Me Out: a 5g solid perfume stick in a flip-top box. Alcohol free. Skin-nourishing. Pocket-sized. ₹249 each, because great scent should not require justification — and no cap on how many you carry.

The box is not packaging. It is the brand's first statement. When you pull it out, people notice before you have applied a single thing. That moment is part of what we sell.

What we stand for

  • Scent as identity, not occasion. CH24 is not for special moments. It is for every moment.
  • Accessible without compromise. Real quality at a price that does not require justification.
  • Genderless by architecture. Every CH24 product is designed for everyone, built into the foundation.
  • India-rooted, world-aware. Scent profiles, pricing, and culture grounded here. Ambition and craft standard: global.

Where it goes

The stick is just the beginning. CH24 is building toward a complete fragrance house that grows from the pocket into every part of how Gen Z experiences scent. All under one identity. All built the same way: accessible, intentional, and unapologetically for this generation.

POLICY

Shipping & returns.

Shipping

We ship across India. Orders are packed and dispatched within 24 to 48 hours of confirmation.

  • Metro cities: usually 2 to 4 working days.
  • Rest of India: usually 4 to 7 working days.

You get a tracking link on WhatsApp or email as soon as your order ships. Shipping is free on all packs.

Returns

Fragrance is personal care, so hygiene rules apply: we can only accept returns of unopened, unused packs with the seal intact.

  • Raise a return within 7 days of delivery via WhatsApp or the contact page.
  • Once we receive and check the pack, your refund is processed to the original payment method within 5 to 7 working days.

Damaged or wrong order

If your pack arrives damaged, or we sent the wrong scents, send us a photo on WhatsApp within 48 hours of delivery. We replace it, no questions, no return shipping cost to you.

Cancellations

You can cancel any order before it ships for a full refund. Once dispatched, it follows the return rules above.

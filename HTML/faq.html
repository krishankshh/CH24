FAQ

Questions, answered.

What is a solid perfume?

A wax-based fragrance you swipe on instead of spray. Take Me Out is a 5g solid perfume stick: alcohol free, skin-nourishing, and small enough to live in your pocket.

How do I apply it?

Flip the box open, pull the stick out, swipe it on pulse points: wrists, neck, behind the ears. Reapply whenever you want. No spray, no cloud, no asking the room's permission.

Is it unisex?

Yes. Every CH24 product is designed for everyone. It is built into the foundation of the brand, not a per-product choice.

What comes in a pack?

Two 5g solid perfume sticks you choose from the four fragrances in Pack 01, for ₹499. Pick your pair here.

Is it safe on skin?

The formula is alcohol free and made to nourish skin, not dry it. If you have known fragrance allergies, patch-test on your wrist first, same as any fragrance.

How long does shipping take?
